Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use your materials for my organization?

Yes! Our educational materials are freely available for non-commercial educational and advocacy purposes. Please provide attribution when using our content.

How can I translate materials into my language?

We welcome translations! Please contact us to discuss the process and ensure consistency with our educational objectives.

Do you provide in-person training?

We can connect you with partner organizations that provide in-person training. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and location.

How can I contribute to your research?

We welcome research collaborations. Please reach out with details about your work, methodology, and how you'd like to contribute.
